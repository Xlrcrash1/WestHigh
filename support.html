Band Boosters

A 'Band Booster' is any parent, guardian, and/or family member of any student participating in any of the Viking Regiment ensembles - Marching Band, Drumline, Color Guard, Orchestra, & Jazz Band.
The Band Booster's purpose is to assist the Band Director and Students in creating and producing the best possible musical programs.

Volunteers

Please consider becoming a volunteer! Volunteers are the foundation of everything we do!
Family and friends are encouraged to become involved however you'd like!

All volunteers work alongside our Boosters on all of the following:

Selling Food & Merchandise @ Football Games • Building Props • Assisting with Uniforms • Fundraising • Moving equipment
Driving the trailer • Running the Potato Booth • Volunteering at the Fireworks Booth • Make meals for the students • Chaperoning on Bus rides and trips
and so much more!
