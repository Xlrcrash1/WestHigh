Band Boosters

A 'Band Booster' is any parent, guardian, and/or family member of any student participating in any of the Viking Regiment ensembles - Marching Band, Drumline, Color Guard, Orchestra, & Jazz Band.
The Band Booster's purpose is to assist the Band Director and Students in creating and producing the best possible musical programs.

Volunteers

Please consider becoming a volunteer! Volunteers are the foundation of everything we do!
Family and friends are encouraged to become involved however you'd like!

All volunteers work alongside our Boosters on all of the following:

Selling Food & Merchandise @ Football Games • Building Props • Assisting with Uniforms • Fundraising • Moving equipment •
Driving the trailer • Running the Potato Booth • Volunteering at the Fireworks Booth • Make meals for the students • Chaperoning on bus rides and trips •
and so much more!

To start Volunteering, please contact Band Director Erin Wander or JOIN in on the Booster's conversation.

Sponsors

Would you like to sponsor the West High School Viking Regiment musical ensembles?!
There are three levels of sponsorships (Color Guard, Drumline, & Viking Regiment) based on the contribution's amount during the band's season (An Academic Year : August-June).

Color Guard Level

$500-$1000 Contribution

  • Framed 8x10 certificate of appreciation with band photo

  • Business Name advertisement on all WHS Viking Regiment Newsletters and Band Programs

  • Two tickets to fall, winter, and spring concerts

Drumline Level

$1000-$1500 Contribution

  • Framed 8x10 certificate of appreciation with band photo and signatures

  • Business logo advertisement on all WHS Viking Regiment Newsletters, Band Programs, and Website

  • Two tickets to fall, winter, and spring concerts

  • Two show t-shirts

Viking Regiment Level

$1500+ Contribution

  • Framed 8x10 certificate of appreciation with band photo and 'thank you' messages with signatures

  • Business logo advertisement on all WHS Viking Regiment Newsletters, Band Programs, Website

  • Business logo on Trailer (2'x 2')

  • Public Thank You announcement of sponsorship during half-time home football games

  • Four tickets to fall, winter, and spring concerts

  • Four show t-shirts

The following details the types of exposure sponsoring the Viking Regiment entails:

Concert Programs

Programs for each of our concerts for Fall, Winter, & Spring are printed and to be considered a keepsake. These prints would include your Company Name/Logo thanking your for your contribution and provide greater visibility within our greater community!

WHS Viking Regiment Website

The WHS Viking Regiment website is a vital platform for anyone desiring to engage further with the Viking Regiment. From former to future members and their families, Faculty, and Staff, the Viking Regiment website is the place to look for any future musical events! your logo, Advertisement, and links can be showcased on our site!

WHS Viking Regiment Trailer

To be proudly seen by Everyone & Everywhere we travel to for any and all performances, from Visalia to Irvine!

Various Events

The Viking Regiment participates in various venus: School and Sporting events, Parades, Fundraisers, a much more! These venues can draw large crowds, ensuring our sponsor's exposure!

We hope you review our benefits and greatly appreciate your consideration in sponsoring the West High School Viking Regiment and ensembles!
