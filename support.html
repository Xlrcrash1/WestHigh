Band Boosters

A 'Band Booster' is any parent, guardian, and/or family member of any student participating in any of the Viking Regiment ensembles - Marching Band, Drumline, Color Guard, Orchestra, & Jazz Band.

The Band Booster's purpose is to assist the Band Director and Students in creating and producing the best possible musical programs.
